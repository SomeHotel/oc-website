## Mossgroves
### Alderick
Her and [[Alderick\|Alderick]] met when they were young children and were close friends up until their late teens where they mutually confessed their love for each other under the moonlight on top of a hill. 

After adventuring for a good amount of their 20s, after a particularly bloody and exhaustive battle, bathed in blood and guts, she throws out a marriage proposal to him. He accepts on the spot, before they both pass out from exhaustion. The next morning, as they're both waking up and they lock eyes, they both burst out laughing after realising they're now fiancés. They stayed fiancés for the next few years due to being busy adventuring.
