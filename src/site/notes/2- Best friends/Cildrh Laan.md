# Background
- His [[Cildrh father\|father]] was harsh on him growing up, especially when it came to swordplay.
- His [[Cildrh mother\|mother]] was very distant, never having showed much interest in having children, only ending up doing so to get his father off her ass.
- He tended to be very boisterous in school, often resulting in detentions or similar punishments. He skipped classes to smoke more often than not and tended to avoid people when possible. When not acting out, he was extremely quiet and had trouble standing up for himself, preferring to outright avoid conflict if he could help it.
